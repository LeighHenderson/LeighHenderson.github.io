--- layout: default title: "Immunocytochemistry" date: 2016-09-01 11:23:12 -0400 categories: protocols --- Immunocytochemistry Purpose: Immunocytochemisty (ICC) stains specific spots in cells so that these spots are visible under a microscope. DAPI is a common stain that marks the nucleus, for example. Immunofluorescence staining uses antibodies to mark the location of target proteins.
Materials: 4% paraformaldehyde

1. In a fume hood, snap the top of a 10ml ampoule of 16% PFA (pull top towards you). Dump the PFA directly from the vial into a 50m l conical.
a.16% PFA ampoules are good indefinitely
2. Add 4ml 10x PBS and 26ml double distilled water
3. Label with “4% paraformaldehyde,” name, date, and expiration dat e (10 days after making). Store at 4C.
